OUR OBJECTIVE

AKDA seeks to bring together Kings and Queens from across the African Continent and her Diaspora to promote Social Cohesion and develop cooperation amongst Traditional African Kingdoms and all People who recognize Africa as their ancestral home or origin. AKDA also ensures that cohesion amongst Africans is in pursuit of full economic, social, cultural, civil as well as political rights of Africans and those of African descent.

Our commitment

To facilitate the integration of Africans and African Diaspora, through the rekindling of African peoples' sense of identity and reconnecting them to the land of their forefathers who were taken into slavery 500 years ago. This process will involve DNA testing that will identify the origins of each person.

AKDA

VISION

To create safe spaces
for engagements, reconnections, integration, solidarity among all people of African descent, with a view to make a lasting contribution towards eradicating all forms of discrimination and violence against Africans as well as build social cohesion and socio-economic prosperity of the African child.
