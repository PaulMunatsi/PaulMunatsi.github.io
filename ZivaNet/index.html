Who We Are

About ZivaNet

A registered non-profit charitable trust dedicated to advancing online safety and digital security for young people across all districts of Zimbabwe.

Our Mission

To establish a non-profit making trust that assists in the development of young people by providing awareness on computer literacy and cyber-security through creating equal opportunities based exclusively on their professional skills, professional training, humanitarian work and creative development in all the Districts of Zimbabwe.

Our Vision

To research, advocate, train, support, and promote online safety and security for youths in Zimbabwe and generate, produce and disseminate innovative ideas and cutting-edge cyber-security policy analysis to advance online safety for young people in Zimbabwe.
