Our Story

Powering Zimbabwe's Future Since Day One

TalivinPower is a Zimbabwean-based technology and energy solutions company dedicated to delivering world-class electrical and solar services. With increasing demand for security, energy efficiency, convenience, and power reliability, we recognized the need for locally-adapted smart solutions.

We specialize in integrated solutions including smart lighting, security systems, energy monitoring, solar integration, access control, and remote property management. All our solutions are tailored to the Zimbabwean environment, addressing power outages, security concerns, and rising energy costs.

From residential homeowners to commercial enterprises, gated communities, real estate developers, offices, retail spaces, hotels, schools, hospitals, and industrial facilities - we serve all sectors with the same commitment to excellence.

Our Mission

To provide customized smart automation systems that enable clients to remotely control lighting, security, appliances, energy consumption, and solar systems. We aim to make smart energy solutions accessible, affordable, and reliable for every Zimbabwean home and business.

Our Vision

To become Zimbabwe's leading smart automation provider, delivering efficient, secure, and energy-smart solutions that transform how people live and work. We envision a future where every property is powered by clean, reliable energy.
