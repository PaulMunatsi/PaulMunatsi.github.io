Privacy Policy

Your privacy, by design

Effective 15 June 2026

At MAD SAFFI, dignity extends to how we handle your information. The short version: we don't collect any of it.

✓ No personal data is collected, stored, or shared
✓ No account or sign-in required to use the app
✓ No tracking, analytics, or cookies of any kind

No user data collection

The MAD SAFFI app does not collect, store, or share any user data. It's an information-sharing app, built to give you content without ever asking you to submit personal information.

No accounts or personal information required

You won't be asked to create an account, provide contact details, or share anything personal to use the app. Every feature is fully accessible without entering any data at all.

No tracking or analytics

The app doesn't use tracking tools, analytics software, or cookies to monitor how you use it. Your interactions stay entirely anonymous.

Data security

Since the app never processes or stores user data, there's no risk of a data breach or unauthorized access to personal information through MAD SAFFI.
